I'm a radiology resident who likes to write about random hobbies in my life and places I've been. This website was originally conceived as a way to organize my [photography portfolio](https://www.codyhou.com/photography/), but has grown to become a representation of the hodge-podge of 50 million interests I have. None of the content written here is created with the assistance of large language models, which I write as a disclaimer because 1) that's probably why my posts don't make much sense, and 2) I naturally use em dashes and semicolons to the point where my writing might be suspect.

Things I'm currently into include: 

* 📷 Photography (35mm film, 6x6 medium format, CCD DSLRs, current DSLRs)
* 🦆 Birding and bird photography
* 🛠️ Fixing and repairing old lenses/cameras
* 🖥️ Homelabbing with Proxmox VE
* 🎮 Baldur's Gate 3, Factorio
* 🎧️ Japanese fusion jazz (Casiopea, T-SQUARE)
